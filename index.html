Learn About Blockchain and Cryptocurrency

Meetings

Our meeting will cover many topic relating to cryptocurrency and blockchain technology. Some of the main focuses will be: Fundamental Analysis, Technical Analysis, Blockchain Development, Blockchain Security, Blockchain Proof Methods/Systems, Cryptocurrency Legalities, and many more.

Our Team

We have a strong and knowledgeable team here at the Crypto & Blockchain Club. Our team is divided up into two main departments, Crypto and Blockchain. The crypto team has club leaders that will focus on technical analysis of the crypto market as well as fundamental analysis. The blockchain department has club leaders that will focus on blockchain and its use in crypto and other application, as well as leaders that focus on the in depths of the technology and the code behined it.

UCC Token

UCC Token was developed by Aric, one of our blockchain leaders, and is the club's official ERC-20 token that runs on the Rinkeby Etherscan Network. The token will be used as a reward/competition system in the club throughout the year, and can be cashed in for prizes such as gift cards, UCC attire, and more!

Meet the Team
